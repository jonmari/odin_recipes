Chicken Franchese

Once this chicken is done cooking, it has the most amazing texture! The inside is moist and tender, while the outside has more of a crisp bite to it. The combination is incredible and makes my mouth water just thinking about it! The lemony sauce brings a fresh, bright taste to the dish, making this recipe one that you cannot miss. Chicken is a dish that the entire family loves, and this chicken Francese is no exception! Plus, it is ready in less than 30 minutes, and you cannot beat that!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Lemon
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • White Wine
  • Chicken broth
  • Parsley
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Heat oil and prepare chicken: Heat the oil in a large skillet. Salt and pepper the chicken. In one bowl add the flour and garlic powder. In another bowl add the eggs.
  2. Dredge the chicken and cook: Dredge the chicken first in the flour and then the eggs. Add it to the hot oil and fry on each side for 2-3 minutes until golden and cooked throughout. Remove and set aside on a plate. Wipe down the skillet with a paper towel.
  3. Make sauce: Add the butter to the pan and melt. Add in the flour and whisk until it starts to thicken. Then, add in the white wine and bring to a boil for a minute.
  4. Add chicken to sauce: Add the chicken broth and lemon juice. Whisk until the sauce has reduced. Return the chicken to the pan and garnish with parsley and sliced lemons.
