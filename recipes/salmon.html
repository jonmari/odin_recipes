Salmon

This recipe was one of our top recipes of 2022 and an enduring,& tried-and-true favorite of Delish readers, and we cannot help but agree: It truly never disappoints. This savory-sweet salmon is quick and easy enough for weeknights (ready in under 30 minutes), but is also fancy enough for at-home date nights (or, an extra special Valentine's Day dinner). If you are a seafood novice, do not sweat it: Searing fish might seem intimidating, but with our top tips, you will be a salmon pro in no time.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Salmon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic powder
  • Parsley
  • red pepper flakes
  • Lemon
  • Low sodium soy sauce

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, lemon juice and red pepper flakes.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat two tablespoons oil. When oil is hot but not smoking, add salmon skin-side up and season with salt and pepper. Cook salmon until deeply golden, about 6 minutes, then flip over and add remaining tablespoon of oil.
  3. Add garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant, 1 minute. Add the honey mixture and sliced lemons and cook until sauce is reduced by about 1/3. Baste salmon with the sauce.
  4. Garnish with sliced lemon and serve.
